About AquaFix Plumbing

Founded in 2010, AquaFix Plumbing has grown to become one of Manchester's most trusted plumbing companies. What started as a small family business has expanded to a team of 12 fully qualified plumbers serving residential and commercial clients across Greater Manchester.

We believe in doing the job right the first time, which is why we invest in ongoing training for our team and use only the highest quality materials. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous industry awards and a loyal customer base.

When you choose AquaFix Plumbing, you're choosing a company that values honesty, integrity, and outstanding workmanship above all else.
